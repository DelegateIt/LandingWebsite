SO I’M IN AUSTIN...WHAT NOW?

Austin is known for its weird culture and it’s a city filled with exciting things to do! We compiled a list of all our favorites just for you. If you have any questions about our suggestions, need directions, or if you simply need a ride, send us a text at (512) 359-3557!

What To See

Mount Bonnell - Just a short walk to one of the best views of Lake Austin. We highly recommend the trip just to see Austin from above. The drive up is incredible too! Located at 3800 Mt Bonnell Rd.

Texas State Capitol - Austin is home to the largest State Capitol in the Nation! Enjoy a walk around and through the building. Located at 1100 Congress Ave.

Baylor Street Graffiti Wall - Baylor Street Wall is iconic for it’s art, and it changes every day. If you’re looking for true Austin culture, this is a must-see on your trip. Located at 11th & Baylor Street.

Zilker Park - Home of the Trail of Lights and ACL Music Festival, Zilker Park always has some exciting event going on. Take a walk through the park, see the Austin Skyline from above, or send us a text and we will see what events are going on now at Zilker! Located at 2100 Barton Springs Rd.

What To Eat

Barbeque - Everyone knows you simply can’t find better BBQ than in the Heart of Texas. Our most famous joints are Franklin’s BBQ, known for its very long wait, and Salt Lick, known for it’s all-you-can-eat brisket. Some other favorites from our team are Salty Sow and Berts Bar-B-Q. No matter where you go, you simply have to try some Texas Ribs!

Pizza - If you are like us, you love a good slice of pizza! The best Pizza joints in Austin are Austin’s Pizza, Homeslice, Conan’s Pizza Central, and Salvation Pizza!

Burgers - You can’t beat a good burger, and you especially can’t beat a good Austin burger! Head on over to HopDoddy for a large juicy burger, and freshly made buns. If you’re in a rush, stop by P. Terrys or Dan’s Burgers, just as good and half the wait!

Desserts - If you’re a fan of the midnight snack, we’ve got some top spots for you here! Amy’s Ice Cream has the best ice cream in town, and Gordough’s Donuts are an adventure on their own!

What To Do

Paddle Boarding - Whether you’re a kayaker or a paddleboarder, Lady Bird Lake is the place to be! Escape that Texas heat with an afternoon on the water. Use the DelegateIt application to get some time on the water!

Alamo Drafthouse - Catch a movie at one of the original movie theaters, located right here in Austin! They have everything from new films to Weird Wednesday, where they play our favorite classics.

Austin Panic Room - It’s an experience like you would not imagine! The Austin Panic Room is an hour long game for up to eight people where you try to escape the room as fast as you can. Our team loves it, and we are sure you will too!

The Thinkery - The museum that is loved by adults AND their kids! The Austin Thinkery is an interactive museum that is an absolute hoot for families.

Craft Pride - When you think of Austin Bars, you typically think of 6th street; few people know that Rainey Street is where the best spots are! Craft Pride is a local favorite. Watch what looks like a little neighborhood turn into a lit-up bar scene as soon as it gets dark, and enjoy the night over at Craft Pride.
